Martinis

Martinis E.S.T. 1930s,were mixed 1part Gin & 1Dry-Vermouth. Later in 40s, became 2/3 Gin & 1 Dry Vermouth.

Now, Martinis bases Gin/Vodka + Dry Vermouth.

We use StrightUp Glass or on the Rocks Glass. Also, Stemmed Glass/Old-Fashioned Glass.
